
# VineLog Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Introduction

VineLog ("the App") is designed to help vineyard owners and growers record, manage, and analyze vineyard activities.

## Information We Collect

The App stores vineyard-related information entered by users, including:

* Parcels and vineyard information
* Activity records
* Harvest records
* Materials and fertilizer usage
* Photos attached to activities (if enabled)

This information is stored locally on your device unless future cloud synchronization features are introduced.

## Location Information

With your permission, VineLog may access your device location in order to provide location-based weather information relevant to vineyard management.

Location data is only used for weather-related functionality and is not used to identify you personally.

## Firebase Analytics

VineLog uses Firebase Analytics to better understand how the application is used and to improve the user experience.

Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous usage information, device information, and application interaction data.

## Firebase Crashlytics

VineLog uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and technical diagnostics that help identify and resolve software issues.

Crash reports may include technical information such as:

* Device model
* Operating system version
* App version
* Stack traces
* Diagnostic information

## Notifications

VineLog may request permission to send notifications related to:

* Vineyard reminders
* Seasonal tasks
* Weather alerts
* Activity recommendations

Notifications can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

## Data Sharing

VineLog does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Some data may be processed by Google Firebase services as described above.

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data.

Vineyard records are primarily stored locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy

VineLog is not intended for children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this document.
